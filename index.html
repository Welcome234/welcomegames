Welcome's Launcher


The Launcher is used to easily install and update our games in one simple application and provides settings for launching games such as setting your username and the character editor.

Our Games

Cube2D

Cube2D is a 2D sandbox game with infinite experience and endless possibilities, and you will need survive and build in a 2D world and fight the bosses to progress in your world. You can build cities, villages, amazing maps and more.


The game also has Multiplayer servers where you can play the game with your friends or other players and have fun in the game together.
